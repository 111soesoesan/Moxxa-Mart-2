## 7. Business Logic

- Variable product price comes from variations
- Cannot publish product without valid pricing
- Inventory handled per variation
- SKU must be unique per shop
- Category supports hierarchy
- Variation image overrides product image when selected
- Product gallery remains shared across all variations

---

## 8. System Integration Updates (CRITICAL)

After implementing this system, update all related logic across the application:

### Inventory System
- Ensure inventory is tracked per variation (not parent product)
- Update stock deduction logic during checkout
- Reflect stock status correctly in product tables and UI

---

### Customer Facing Product Page
- When a user selects attributes (e.g. color, size):
  - Update displayed image using variation image
  - Update price based on selected variation
  - Update stock availability dynamically

- If no variation is selected:
  - Show default product image
  - Disable purchase or prompt selection

---

### Order System
- Ensure orders store selected variation data
- Include variation attributes in order items

---

### Search and Filtering
- Ensure variable products are searchable by attributes
- Prepare for future filtering by attributes (color, size, etc.)

---

### Data Consistency
- Ensure no orphan variations
- Ensure variation combinations are unique
- Validate all required fields before activation
